Sometimes, when viewing a property, you need to show proof of funds. So-called proof of funds is really just opening your bank account and letting them take a quick look to show you have the financial strength.

I really want to view the property, but apparently I need 5 million RMB in my bank account to do that. Unfortunately, I do not have 5 million. Fortunately, I can vibe code.

This static app is a 1:1 replica of a real banking app—Bank of Hangzhou. I only recreated the home page and the balance display page. It is not interactive, but it does include a fingerprint login screen. It is good enough for a quick glance when proof of funds is needed. It also lets me experience what it feels like to have 5 million RMB in my account.
